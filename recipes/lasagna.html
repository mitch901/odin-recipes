Index Lasagna recipe

Finished lasagna dish

Traditionally an Italian dish, lasagna is a flat and expanded pasta sheet covered in parmesan sauce, white sauce and ragu, a meat based sauce. Despite its traditional form, lasagna has been altered over the years and consists of many different ways of making the dish.

The word lasagna (plural lasagne) actually refers to the pasta itself, with the finished dish actully being called "pasta al forno"

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive olive
  • 1 pound ground beef chunks
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (3/4 cups)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) good quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 (14-ounce) crushed tomatos
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic pouder
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook bell pepper, onions and garlic; add back the beef
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Boil and drain lasagna pasta
  6. Preheat oven
  7. Assemble lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
